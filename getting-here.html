Getting Here

Welcome to Rosas Garden Hotel: Where Hospitality Blooms

Via Metered Taxi

From any location in Metro Manila, ask the driver to take you to Rosas Garden Hotel beside Ermita Church.

Via GRAB Car

From any location in Metro Manila, use GRAB App to book a vehicle going to the hotel.

Search and Pin Rosas Garden Hotel as your destination.

From the Airport (Ninoy Aquino International Airport or NAIA Terminals 1, 2, 3 and 4)

Via Metered Taxi
  • From the Airport Taxi Bay, you may get a cab going to Rosas Garden Hotel
  • Taxi fare ranges PHP 250 and above (Taxi fare depends on the duration of travel).
Via GRAB Car

See above

How to Get to Rosas Garden Hotel

Here’s a Guide on how you can go to Rosas Garden Hotel via public commuting:

If you are coming from Monumento Area:
  • Ride a Jeepney bound to Baclaran ( via Mabini). Ask the Driver to alight you off at U.N. Avenue corner Arquiza Street (Cowboy Grill Mabini). From there you can just walk to Rosas Garden Hotel.
  • Ride the LRT-1. Alight off at U.N. Avenue Station. From there, you can just walk or ride a Pedicab going to Rosas Garden Hotel.
If you are coming from Baclaran Area:
  • Ride a Jeepney bound to Divisoria or Monumento (via Mabini). Ask the Driver to alight you off at U.N. Avenue corner Arquiza Street (Cowboy Grill Mabini) from there, you can just walk going to Rosas Garden Hotel.
  • Ride the LRT-1. Alight off at U.N. Avenue Station. From there, you can just walk or ride a Pedicab going to Rosas Garden Hotel.

Via Private Vehicle

If you are bringing your own vehicle, you may follow driving directions from Waze or Google Maps. However, parking is limited and on a first come – first serve basis.
