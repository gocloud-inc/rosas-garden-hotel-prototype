Welcome to Rosas Garden Hotel

Rosas Garden Hotel would like to welcome you to the Entertainment center!

A hotel that is proud to be able to quench the desires of all types of people, it makes the ideal escape for both the business travelers and leisure guests alike.

Rosas Garden Hotel has emerged to be one of the most preferred standard hotels in Manila, Philippines. All rooms are tailored fit to meet and surpass your expectations, with its professional and highly trained personnel in meeting every specific need of our clients with warm smiles, distinct efficiency and service that come from the heart.

Rosas Garden Hotel is a corporation duly registered under Philippine laws, and accredited by the Philippine Department of Tourism.

Location

Rosas Garden Hotel is conveniently located at the Heart of Historical City of Manila, the gateway to the Islands of the Philippines.

We are practically 30 minutes away from both Manila International and Domestic Airports and 15 minutes away from Manila International and Domestic Sea Ports.

Rosas Garden Hotel is a 24-hotel room, relatively small, where every guest receives our personalized service. Every guest gets to know each other pretty well. It is your home away from home at the heart of Manila. That is why our guests keep on coming back.

Whether you are here for business, pleasure or in transit, Rosas Garden Hotel is the best place for you to stay while in Manila. Rosas Garden Hotel is ideally located at the Heart of Historic City of Manila, the gateway to the Islands of the Philippines. We are practically 30 minutes away from both Manila International and Domestic Airports and 15 minutes away from Manila International and Domestic Sea Ports.

City of Manila, being the Capital City of the Republic of the Philippines, it is here where most Government and Private offices are located. We are just a stone throw away from the U.S. Embassy, World Health Organization, and most of the Philippine Government offices and Multi-National Main offices such as the Philippine Department of Tourism, the Philippine Supreme Court, Court of Appeals and the Land Bank of the Philippines to name a few.

Rizal Park, Philippine's National Park, the popular Bay Walk, where you will find Philippines famous Sun Set, the Greens of Intramuros Golf Course, Cultural Center of the Philippines Complex, must see tourist destinations, are all in walking distance.

For people who love shopping, we are just a 10 minute walk away from Robinson's Mall, one of the Philippines biggest malls. While for people who love thrill we are also 10 minutes away from two Casinos.
